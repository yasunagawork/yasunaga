inspiration

start with my inspiration of project

  1. Prosthetics. Arifitial limbs and body that faciliate new way of interfacing with world. Midified body image helps to see fluidity in our idea about our body.

  2. Where do our body start and end? Where do I start and end? While any object participate in our thoughts and view of "world" isn't object me as much as my body? I am making tools see possibility in having a multiple ..or multi dimentional body

  3. Tuning device: Like tuning a guitar, provided reference point(object) help us orient our body and thoughts. It provides structure for our expression wich have a segnificant effect on what and how we express. Using different tuning device will give new work flow of calibration and new tuning will restructure our relationship between body movement and notes, body of instrunment.

  4. Making our action un predictable: I am interested in objects that takes some agency away from our ability to controll. While retaining half of agency in controll, have objects and tool participate in active markmaking. My body and tool colaborate. Good craftman uses tool as part of their body and it becomes invisible. Being a good craftsman is not a concern here. It is to have tool be more expressive and participate in our expression

  5. Blue Apron: We are at the age of packaged identity and life style. Guided meditation and urganomic chair. Starter pack provides step by step on what to do in our lives so we can suceed. Starter pack frame a subject carefully so it is ateinable and acsessible. I am interested this dilenma in the nature of starter pack and its powerfull structuring capability

  6. Aftermarket parts: Aftermarket parts are ofsetted interest and enhanced, systhesised aesthetics for specific needs and wants. Synonems and Dipping sause. Aftarmarket parts realizes product in unchallenged field and context. It pushes products to unpredictable identity (devil and angel)

Materials and Tools for this class

here are list of materials you will need to get for the first half of the semester. Second half of the semester there will be minimal required purchases.

  • Arduino Uno R3 (25 ~ $30)
  • USB cord A to B (one that is commonly used on printers)(3 ~ $5)
  • 400+ pin Breadboard (prototyping board) (5 ~ $8)
  • Jumper wire kit(preformed or flexible)
  • Small storage container for your projects

There are many clones of Arduino and they might be a little cheaper, but for the sake of ease and consistency let's make sure we all get Arduino Uno R3. It will probably save you some troubles...

Here are some places to buy Arduino:

  • Amazon prime is free for 6 months for students
  • Arduino.ccArduino official website.
  • Adafruit Close in NYC, generally fast shipping
  • Sparkfun shipping can be slow

Be sure to get the USB A-B cable!
