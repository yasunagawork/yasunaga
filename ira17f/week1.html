HOMEWORK

PREPARE SHORT INTRODUCTORY PRESENTATION

The short presentation you give next week will consist of two parts:

  1. A self-introduction in which you show some things you are currently interested in.
  2. 1 or 2 rough ideas of something you'd like to work on during the first half of this semester.

DETAILS

  • Total length: 5-10 minutes.
  • Format: Anything goes (Powerpoint/Google Slides, InDesign/Illustrator/Word document, webpage, video, stack of files) as long as the material is coherently organized! You must arrive to class prepared.
  • Tone: Casual but serious. Think hard about what you're showing, but you don't need to script everything out or use cue cards.
  • Due: Next week at 9am (Mandatory attendance. Don't be late!)

CONTENT

  1. Research 2 things that represent some of your current interests. This might be the creative work of others (art/design/games/whatever) or something else you find personally inspiring. Do these things currently inform your creative practice in some sort of way?
  2. What would you like to make next? You can be general or specific about this. Try not to think too hard about the future, think about right now. What excites you? What's interesting? What seems enjoyable?
  3. Find 2 examples of work that has already been done (by someone else) which is somehow related to what you'd like to make. Why these particular things?
  4. Anything else you think is personally relevant. Don't feel like you have to fill the full 10 minutes... you don't! :)
