Transportation

Airport

Fukuoka Airport(FUK) is approximately a 45 minute drive from Miyawaka and offers many connections both internationally and domestically.

To get to Fukuoka, you will most likely have a layover in Korea or elsewhere in Japan and we recommend allowing plenty of time if transferring in Tokyo. Please also be mindful that there are 2 different airports in Tokyo and they are not close (so make sure your layover doesn’t require you to switch airports!).

Getting to Miyawaka City

Fukuoka airport has a variety of rental car options and a subway station to take you into the city. Fukuoka’s main train station (called Hakata) offers connections to many other destinations throughout Japan.

Hakata station to Akama station on train

Please note: More details about wedding transportation will follow, but we recommend you to rent a car while you’re in the area to be moble. We do suggest you take public transportation if traveling elsewhere in Japan.
