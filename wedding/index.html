Transportation

Airport

Fukuoka Airport(FUK) is approximately a 45 minute drive from Miyawaka and offers many connections both internationally and domestically.

Getting to Miyawaka City

Fukuoka airport has a variety of rental car options and a subway station to take you into the city. Fukuoka’s main train station (called Hakata) offers connections to many other destinations throughout Japan.

Togo Station is the closest train station to Munakata Shrine and Chisun Inn Munakata and Akama Station is the closest train station to Lucas's family's home.

Hakata station to Akama station on train

Wedding Day Transportation

We recommend that you rent a car while you’re in the Miyawaka area to be mobile. There is free parking at both wedding venues. We will also provide a shuttle to Nansuikaku Ryokan for the dinner reception, with more details to come.

FAQ

Dress code

Please dress nicely (semi-formal) but comfortably. The ceremony will be undercover but mostly outside and it may be cold in the March weather. The reception will be shoe-less so keep this in mind when planning footwear.

Gifts

Tradition in Japan is to give cash gifts (goshugi) to the couple at their wedding. Please don't feel obligated to give us any gifts, as your presence is the greatest gift!

Onsen (hot springs)

Onsen are a very important part of Japanese culture and one of our favorite aspects of Japan. They are enjoyed totally naked. You are not obligated to go in the hot springs and there are private hot springs available if you are interested but uncomfortable with nudity.
