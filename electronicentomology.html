Electronic Entomology

Electronic Entomology is the study that explores the nuances of behaviors and characteristics of solar-powered electronic insects. This project explore electronic insects as a cohabitant and satellite senses to experience our surroundings in a new way. The web archive functions for the exploration of classifications of these insects and materials for the visitors to build them on their own.

Project website: http://electronicentomology.com
