Transient - Composition 1


This is a compositional work that demostrate communication between electronic solar bugs and household appliances. Transients are a new speices of electronic being that interpretes the patterns of solar bugs to the appliances. Transients are mediators. They are an translators and deplomat. They are signle sinapse that make new connections. Conditioning applinces to be sensetive and volnerble. Solar bugs are tiny. They creates patterns of charp based on amount of light they get. Fluctuation voltage between 0V and 2V. Household appliance like printers and vaccume cleaners, they are larger in size and wants constant voltage of 120V and 220V. Transients cling on to the solar bugs with it's tenticles, listening closely of how they speak. Than transients inturrupt the 220V in a pattern of how the solar bug spoke, allowing solar bug to posess the appliances. Composition made here consists of
