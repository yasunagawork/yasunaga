Environment - playground for musical ecosystem


Environemnt

Environment is a playground for musical ecosystem which player collaborates with various synthetic animals with limited receptors and effectors. Environment is an architectural model and arena, the site, zone, the theatrical stage for an explorative system making for dynamic relationships between the player and these beings.
