PEER REVIEW

Everyone is responsible for giving anonymous written feedback for each project. This feedback will be turned in at the end of class and factored into the final evaulation of the each project.

I will redistribute the feedback after Spring Break, so please write legibly and use individual pieces of paper.

Please address the following, giving a brief explanation for each:

  1. Does the project seem finished? (1-5 scale. 1: a lot of unresolved areas, 5: completely finished) Explain:
  2. Overall, how successfully was the project realized? Did it seem to meet its intended goals? (1-5 scale. 1: not sucessful, 5: very sucessful) Explain:
  3. How well was the project built? Does it demonstrate thoroughness and attention to detail? Rate the project's craft (1-5 scale. 1: poorly built, 5: very well made). Explain:
  4. What is the most successful aspect of the project for you?
  5. What is one area that you think could be improved?
  6. What overall grade would you give this project? Explain:
  7. Additional comments? (Optional)
