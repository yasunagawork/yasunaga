HOMEWORK

PROJECT DEVELOPMENT (6 HOURS)

Begin working on your project. This might be...

  1. More brainstorming until you have a direction to move in for the first project.
  2. Continue researching precedents and technical needs.
  3. Prepare materials or gather supplies.
  4. Start working. Play around with the materials. Begin preliminary prototypes. Get your hands dirty. Hit the ground running!

Next week is a studio session, so make sure that you come prepared to do something constructive (and project related!) with that time. I'll also be checking progress, so make sure that you can demonstrate the expected amount of time/effort.em>
